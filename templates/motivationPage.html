Why Meme?

Each culture has their unique meme. Things that people laugh about and relate to in a particular region is different to those in another regions. By understanding memes from other parts of the world, we are able to share a tiny little bit of understanding... and hopefully a little laugh :)

P.S. You can also detect and translate images with text that are not memes! For example, an image of a billboard, restaurant menu, recipe, etc!
